Horse

The horse is a four-legged mammal, widely known for its strong links to wars, literary and poetic writings, because of the beauty of its shape, height, strength and speed.

  • Scientific Name: Equus caballus
  • Average Length: 2.4 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 25-30 years
  • Habitat: Tropical regions and stables

The horse is an animal used for riding, running and racing. Their shapes, sizes, speed and capacity vary according to their type and origin. The average horse length is 2.4 meters.
